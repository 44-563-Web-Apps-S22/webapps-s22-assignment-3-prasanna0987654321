ROUTE

The airport that is closest to your food location in Hyderabad is the Rajiv Gandhi International Airport. The airport is located at a distance of about 20 kilometers from the city center and it is served by 4 airlines. It has been in operation since 2010 and it has a capacity of 2.5 million passengers per year. The airport has 3 terminals, with Terminal 1 being the largest of them all.. The steps for traveling to your food location from the airport in Hyderabad are as follows:

  1. Take a cab to the nearest metro station. The closest metro station is called Nampally. It is located at a distance of 3 kilometers from the airport.
  2. Take the metro train to Kukatpally and then take another bus or cab to your destination, which will take approximately 1 hour and 45 minutes. The fare will cost you around Rs 200-300 per person, depending on how many people are traveling with you.
